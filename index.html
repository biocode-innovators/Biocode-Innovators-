Flagship Internship Programs

Comprehensive, hands-on syllabus tracks engineered to transition students directly into practical application.

Data Analytics Internship

Batch 1 & 2 Course Layout

  • Advanced Excel Formulas & Interface
  • Messy Data Cleaning & Wrangling
  • Interactive Storytelling Dashboards (Slicers & Pivots)
  • Statistical Functions (ANOVA, Linear Regression)
  • Power BI Essentials, Power Query & DAX Basics
Focus: Excel, Power BI, and Dashboard Reporting

Structural Bioinformatics & Sequence Analysis

Batch 1 & 2 Course Layout

  • Database Querying & Formats (NCBI, UniProt, PDB)
  • Pairwise & Local Sequence Alignment (BLAST, FASTA)
  • Multiple Sequence Alignment & Trees (Clustal, MEGA-X)
  • 3D Structure Visualization & Modeling (PyMOL)
  • Rigorous Structure Validation (Ramachandran, PROCHECK)
Focus: Sequence Analysis, Alignment & Macromolecular Modeling
